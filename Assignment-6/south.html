SOUTH-ISLNAD

Kaikoura, situated about halfway between Christchurch and Picton on the east coast of the South Island,
offers a unique combination of ocean and mountains. The stunning coastal alpine scenery includes a host of eco-tourism activities including whale watching,
dolphin swimming and seal snorkelling.
Walk the Kaikoura Peninsula Walkway and discover seal and bird colonies along the coast.
